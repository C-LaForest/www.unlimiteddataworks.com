Pre-Call Research Questions

To make our conversation as productive as possible, please consider these questions:

  • What is the primary focus of your current research projects?
  • What is your annual research technology budget or grant allocation?
  • Do you have dedicated IT support, or are you managing technology independently?
  • What specific compliance requirements do you need to meet (HIPAA, IRB, FDA)?
  • Are you experiencing any data fragmentation or analysis pipeline issues?
  • Do you collaborate with other institutions or manage multi-site studies?

These questions help me understand your unique research environment and tailor our discussion to your specific needs and challenges.
